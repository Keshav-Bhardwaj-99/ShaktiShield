Resources & Support

Helplines & Hotlines

  • **National Women's Helpline:** 1091 (India) - 24/7 support for women in distress.
  • **Childline India:** 1098 - For children in need of care and protection.
  • **Cyber Crime Helpline (India):** 1930 / 155260 - For reporting cyber crimes against women and children.
  • **Women's Safety Cell / Police Helpline:** 112 (All India Emergency) - Integrated emergency services.

Legal Aid & Rights

  • **National Legal Services Authority (NALSA) (India):** Provides free legal services to the weaker sections of society.
  • **State Legal Services Authorities (SLSAs):** Contact your state's legal services authority for local support.
  • **District Legal Services Authorities (DLSAs):** For legal aid at the district level.
  • **Women's Rights Advocates:** Connect with NGOs and legal organizations specializing in women's rights in India.
  • **Understanding Your Rights:** Information on laws related to domestic violence, sexual harassment, and assault under Indian Penal Code (IPC) and other relevant acts.

Counseling & Mental Health

  • **Government Counseling Centers:** Many state governments offer free or subsidized counseling services.
  • **NGOs & Support Groups:** Organizations like Sneha (Chennai) or Sangath (Goa) offer mental health support. Search for local NGOs in your city.
  • **Online Counseling Platforms (India-based):** Look for platforms that connect you with licensed therapists in India.
  • **Trauma-Informed Care:** Seek out mental health professionals in India specializing in trauma recovery.

Self-Defense & Safety Tips

  • **Local Self-Defense Academies:** Many cities in India offer self-defense classes specifically for women.
  • **Police-led Self-Defense Programs:** Some police departments conduct workshops for women's safety.
  • **Awareness & Prevention:** Stay vigilant, avoid isolated areas, and trust your instincts.
  • **Safety Apps (Indian Context):** Apps like 'Himmat Plus' (Delhi Police), 'Raksha', or 'VithU' can be useful for emergencies.
  • **Emergency Preparedness:** Share your live location with trusted contacts, keep emergency numbers on speed dial, and know your nearest safe spots.
